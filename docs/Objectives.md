# Project Objectives: Global Cross-Border Marketplace Platform

## 1. Overall Objective
The primary objective of this project is to **design and develop a full-stack global marketplace platform** that simplifies cross-border buying and selling between India and international markets by improving price transparency, affordability, and product discovery.

The project aims to solve real-world challenges faced by users and small sellers in international e-commerce through thoughtful system design and modern web technologies.

---

## 2. User-Centric Objectives
These objectives focus on improving the experience of both Indian and international users.

* **Global Discovery:** Enable Indian users to discover and purchase global products that are not easily available locally.
* **Authentic Access:** Enable international users to access authentic Indian products directly from verified sellers.
* **Price Clarity:** Reduce confusion and uncertainty by showing the **final landing price** before checkout.
* **Affordability:** Make international shopping more affordable through **group-buying mechanisms**.
* **User Experience:** Provide a simple and intuitive user experience for cross-border transactions.

---

## 3. Functional Objectives
These objectives define the core capabilities of the platform.

* **Landing Price Calculator:** Implement a calculator that clearly displays:
    * Base product price
    * Currency conversion
    * International shipping cost
    * Estimated customs duties and taxes
* **Group Buying:** Allow users to create or join group purchases to share logistics costs.
* **Two-Way Discovery:** Support product flow in both directions:
    * Global products $\rightarrow$ Indian users
    * Indian products $\rightarrow$ Global users
* **Localization:** Provide multilingual support to serve users from different regions.
* **Seller Onboarding:** Enable seller registration and product listing with basic verification.

---

## 4. Cost Optimization Objectives
Reducing cost is a core focus of the project.

* **Logistics Efficiency:** Minimize per-user international shipping costs using bulk and group ordering.
* **Duty Transparency:** Reduce unexpected expenses by calculating duties and fees in advance.
* **Informed Decisions:** Encourage informed purchase decisions through transparent pricing.

---

## 5. Technical Objectives
These objectives focus on system design and engineering goals.

* **Scalability:** Build a scalable full-stack web application using modern technologies.
* **Architecture:** Design a modular and maintainable architecture.
* **Security:** Ensure secure handling of user data and transactions.
* **Extensibility:** Support future expansion through clean APIs and extensible components.
* **Skill Demonstration:** Demonstrate real-world full-stack development skills.

---

## 6. Academic and Learning Objectives
As a **Project-Based Learning (PBL)** initiative, the project also aims to:

* Apply theoretical knowledge to a real-world problem.
* Understand challenges in global e-commerce systems.
* Gain experience in system planning, design, and implementation.
* Improve problem-solving and software engineering skills.
* Create a portfolio-ready project with practical relevance.

---

## 7. Success Criteria
The project will be considered successful if it:

* ✅ Clearly demonstrates how cross-border shopping challenges are addressed.
* ✅ Provides transparent pricing before purchase.
* ✅ Shows cost reduction through group buying.
* ✅ Supports two-way global commerce.
* ✅ Is well-documented and easy to understand for reviewers.

---

## 8. Scope Alignment
These objectives are intentionally designed to:
* Be achievable within a PBL timeframe.
* Reflect real industry problems.
* Allow future feature expansion.
